ASTROVEDA



Daily Poojas

Sandhya Vandanam:
A daily ritual performed at sunrise and sunset to honor the Sun God and maintain spiritual discipline.
Ganapati Pooja:
A simple daily worship of Lord Ganesha for removing obstacles and ensuring success.
Tulasi Pooja:
Worshiping the sacred Tulasi plant, believed to bring peace and prosperity to the home.

Deity-Specific Poojas

Lakshmi Pooja:
Dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi, performed for wealth and prosperity, especially during Diwali.
Shiva Pooja:
Performed to seek blessings from Lord Shiva for health, happiness, and moksha.
Vishnu Pooja:
Worship of Lord Vishnu to invoke protection and stability.

Festival Poojas

Navaratri Pooja:
A nine-day festival dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga and her various forms.
Ganesh Chaturthi Pooja:
Celebrating Lord Ganesha's birth, symbolizing new beginnings and wisdom.
Saraswati Pooja:
Performed during Vasant Panchami or Navaratri to seek blessings for knowledge and learning.

Vedic And Special Occasion Poojas

Satyanarayana Pooja:
A ritual dedicated to Lord Vishnu, often performed during housewarming, weddings, or special milestones.
Navagraha Pooja:
Worship of the nine planetary deities to reduce the effects of planetary doshas (afflictions).
Rudra Abhishekam:
A powerful ritual for Lord Shiva, involving the offering of sacred substances like milk, honey, and ghee.
